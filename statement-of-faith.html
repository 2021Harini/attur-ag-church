OUR FAITH

Assemblies of God Fundamental Truths

The following sixteen Fundamental Truths summarize the essential biblical beliefs of the Assemblies of God.

<

WE BELIEVE... The Scriptures are Inspired by God and declare His design and plan for mankind.

WE BELIEVE... There is only One True God revealed in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit (commonly known as the Trinity).

WE BELIEVE... In the Deity of the Lord Jesus Christ. As God's Son, Jesus was both human and divine.

WE BELIEVE... Though originally good, man willingly fell into sin—ushering evil and death, both physical and spiritual, into the world.

WE BELIEVE... Every person can have restored fellowship with God through salvation by trusting Jesus Christ through faith and repentance as personal Savior.

WE BELIEVE... The Church practices two ordinances: Water Baptism by immersion after salvation, and Holy Communion as a remembrance of Christ's suffering and death.

WE BELIEVE... The Baptism in the Holy Spirit is a special experience following salvation that empowers believers for witnessing and effective Christian service.

WE BELIEVE... The initial physical evidence of the Baptism in the Holy Spirit is speaking in other tongues as the Spirit gives utterance.

WE BELIEVE... Sanctification begins at salvation and continues throughout the believer's life as we grow closer to Christ and separate ourselves from sin.

WE BELIEVE... The Church is the Body of Christ and exists to worship God, proclaim the Gospel, make disciples, and bring people into fellowship with Him.

WE BELIEVE... Divine healing is provided through Christ's atoning sacrifice and is available for believers who trust in Him.

WE BELIEVE... Jesus Christ will return for His Church, and all believers will be caught up to be with the Lord forever.

WE BELIEVE... Jesus Christ will return with His saints to reign on earth for one thousand years, bringing righteousness, peace, and justice.

WE BELIEVE... Those who reject Christ will face final judgment and eternal separation from God, while believers will receive everlasting life.

WE BELIEVE... God will create a new heaven and a new earth where all who have accepted Jesus Christ will live with Him forever in His eternal kingdom.
