About ATTUR AG CHURCH

Sharing God's Love, Building Faith and Serving Our Community.

OUR HISTORY

Our Church Journey

ATTUR AG CHURCH has been faithfully serving the community by preaching the Gospel, nurturing believers, and extending God's love to everyone. Our desire is to build a Christ-centered church where people experience hope, faith, and transformation.

Through worship, prayer, and service, we continue to grow together as one family in Christ.

OUR PURPOSE

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To build a Christ-centered church that transforms lives through faith, worship, and God's Word.

Our Mission

To preach the Gospel, disciple believers, serve the community, and spread God's love to every generation.

OUR BELIEFS

What We Believe

God's Word

We believe the Bible is the inspired and living Word of God.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is our Savior and Lord who gives eternal life.

Prayer

We believe prayer strengthens our relationship with God and one another.
